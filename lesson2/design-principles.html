Contrast

Priorbank - priorbank.by

Contrast is mostly used to emphasise the difference between selected elements, and is created when two elements are very different. Contrast creates visual interest for the user. In this example, the logo itself uses black and yellow contrast, and yellow buttons attract attention to more interesting or important objects for the user.

Rule of Thirds

Fitness club Adrenalin - adrenalin-fitness.by

The rule of thirds is a method of breaking up a design into different sections using columns and rows that form a grid. In this example we see that the screen is visually divided into three rows.

Repetition

Poizon - poizon.com

The use of repetition across a website helps to unify a layout, and ensure consistency. Repetition can be obvious or subtle. In this example, a colour scheme and fonts are repeated. Using the same, consistent design builds trust with the user, is visually appealing, and helps to percept information.
