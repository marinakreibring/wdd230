Overview Audience Design

Target Audience

One part of the audience are current and prospective members of a Chamber of Commerce that are usually international and local operating companies, such as lawyers, property developers, tourism companies, airlines, manufacturing companies, import and export businesses, banks, finance companies, legal advisors, IT and electronics manufacturers etc. Another part of the audience are ordinary citizens and visitors of the city who are interested in city development. Business owners get an opportunity to connect with each other to help grow the community. Visitors can learn about demographics and sights of the city.

Personas

Persona 1

Jorg Kunze, businessman, owner of a small trading company. He is 45 years old, married, has 3 children. He has a net of shops, and he wants to find partner businesses for selling their products in his shops.

Persona 2

Emma Schulz, recently graduated student. She is 23 years old, single, no children. She studied Applied Businsess Management, and now wants to find opportunities for employment in business companies of the city.

Scenarios

  • A visitor of the city wants to know what is the city known for.
  • An owner of a small business wants to find partners and expand his business.
  • A person is investigating business opportunities of the city.
  • A member of the Chamber wants to find current information about its work.
  • Local school wants to find sponsors for its programs.
